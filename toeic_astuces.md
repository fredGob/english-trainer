# Astuces TOEIC — Grammaire

## 1. Les temps verbaux — mots indices

| Mot indice | Temps à utiliser | Exemple |
|---|---|---|
| yesterday, last week, ago | Simple past | *He signed the contract yesterday* |
| tomorrow, soon, next week | Simple future (will) | *She will review the report* |
| already, just, since, for | Present perfect | *I have sent the email* |
| now, currently, at the moment | Present continuous | *She is attending the meeting* |
| by the time, by Friday, before... | Future perfect (will have + pp) | *They will have finished by Friday* |

---

## 2. Nom / Adjectif / Adverbe

Regarde ce qui précède ou suit le blanc :

| Position | Forme à utiliser | Exemple |
|---|---|---|
| Avant un nom | **Adjectif** | *a **decisive** action* |
| Après un verbe d'action | **Adverbe** | *she acted **decisively*** |
| Sujet ou objet de la phrase | **Nom** | *the **decision** was made* |

---

## 3. For / Since / Until / By

| Mot | Suivi de | Sens | Exemple |
|---|---|---|---|
| **for** | durée | pendant | *for ten years* |
| **since** | point de départ | depuis | *since 2015* |
| **until** | moment | jusqu'à (continuité) | *until Friday* |
| **by** | deadline | au plus tard | *by Friday* |

---

## 4. Le passif — by

Au passif, on utilise toujours **by** pour indiquer qui fait l'action.

> *The contract **was signed by** the CEO.*

---

## 5. Despite / Although / However / Because of

| Mot | Suivi de | Sens |
|---|---|---|
| **Despite / In spite of** | nom | malgré |
| **Although / Even though** | phrase complète | bien que |
| **However** | nouvelle phrase | cependant |
| **Because of** | nom | à cause de |
| **Because / Since** | phrase complète | parce que |

**Exemple :**
- *Despite the rain, ...* (nom)
- *Although it was raining, ...* (phrase)

---

## 6. All / Every / Each / Whole

| Mot | Suivi de | Exemple |
|---|---|---|
| **all** | pluriel | *all employees* |
| **every / each** | singulier | *every employee* |
| **whole** | the + singulier | *the whole company* |

---

## 7. Verbes + -ing ou + to ?

### Verbes toujours suivis de **-ing**
> suggest, recommend, avoid, consider, enjoy, mind, finish, keep

*She suggested **postponing** the meeting.*

### Verbes toujours suivis de **to + verbe**
> want, decide, plan, agree, refuse, offer, expect, need

*He decided **to cancel** the trip.*

### Verbes suivis du **verbe seul** (sans to)
> let, make, help (parfois)

*The manager let him **leave** early.*

---

## 8. Prépositions figées fréquentes au TOEIC

| Expression | Préposition |
|---|---|
| responsible ___ | **for** |
| interested ___ | **in** |
| depend ___ | **on** |
| apply ___ | **for** |
| agree ___ | **with** (personne) / **on** (sujet) |
| consist ___ | **of** |
| refer ___ | **to** |

---

## 9. Tell / Say

| Verbe | Structure | Exemple |
|---|---|---|
| **tell** | tell + personne (sans *to*) | *She told me the news* |
| **say** | say + to + personne / say something | *She said to me that...* |

> *She said me...* ❌ — *said us* ❌

---

## 10. Questions indirectes

Après *"Can you tell me / Do you know / I wonder..."* → **pas d'inversion sujet-verbe**

| Direct | Indirect |
|---|---|
| *Where do you come from?* | *Can you tell me where **you come from**?* |
| *What time is it?* | *Do you know what time **it is**?* |

---

## 11. Modaux au passé

| Forme | Sens | Exemple |
|---|---|---|
| **should have + pp** | regret / reproche | *We should have pushed harder* |
| **shouldn't have + pp** | n'aurait pas dû | *We shouldn't have left* |
| **must have + pp** | déduction logique | *He must have forgotten* |
| **could have + pp** | possibilité non réalisée | *She could have called* |

---

## 12. Expressions figées — to + -ing

Ces expressions sont suivies de **to** puis **-ing** (pas l'infinitif) :

| Expression | Exemple |
|---|---|
| **look forward to** | *I look forward to **hearing** from you* |
| **be used to** | *She is used to **working** late* |
| **object to** | *He objected to the **proposal*** |
| **go on + -ing** | *They went on **making** a noise* (= continuer à) |

---

## 13. Vocabulaire clé

### Voyage
| Mot | Sens |
|---|---|
| **journey** | trajet, voyage (chemin parcouru) |
| **trip** | voyage aller-retour |
| **travel** | voyager (verbe) / les voyages (indénombrable) |
| **voyage** | voyage en mer |

### Business
| Mot | Sens |
|---|---|
| **supplier** | fournisseur (produits/pièces) |
| **provider** | fournisseur (services) |
| **purchaser / buyer** | acheteur |
| **vendor** | vendeur/fournisseur |

### Adjectifs — collocations
| Mot | Contexte |
|---|---|
| **wrong** | wrong number, wrong address, wrong answer |
| **false** | false alarm, false information |
| **fake** | fake passport, fake smile |
| **bad** | bad weather, bad news, bad quality |

---

## 14. Comparatifs — renforcement

| Forme | Sens | Exemple |
|---|---|---|
| **much / far / a lot** + comparatif | bien plus | *much faster, far cheaper* |
| **far more** + adjectif | bien plus | *far more expensive* |
| **the most** | superlatif (3+ éléments) | *the most expensive* |

---

## 15. Expressions temporelles figées

| Français | Anglais |
|---|---|
| hier soir | **last night** ✅ (*yesterday night* ❌) |
| hier matin | **yesterday morning** |
| la semaine dernière | **last week** |
| le mois dernier | **last month** |

---

## 16. Voeux + someone + to do

Verbes qui suivent le schéma **verbe + someone + to do** :

> want, ask, expect, need, tell, allow, prefer

- *He wants **me to call** the supplier.*
- *Do you prefer **us to wait**?*
